One to One Healing Sessions

Offered for anyone wishing to explore imbalances and challenges, there is no room for people who are not willing to take this seriously or give it the respect it deserves (which is a lot different from questioning it, that's fine!) These challenges that need to be overcome, are done in a context of remedial energies. Sitting down going through problems at the soul, mental, emotional and physical levels. Helping unpick tangled threads of old stories, embrace our Shadow selves restoring our power and creates a new destiny. Energetic shifts and endowed into physical reality through a process mythic happening, the sessions usually face to face or remotely, full support is given after and between sessions if needed.
