Sharing the Ancient Wisdom of The Medicine Wheel Teachings. Honouring and exploring the sacred energy practises of the Medicine men and women of the Q”euro lineage.

Our relationship with The Earth, ourselves, and our community are critical to our health and well being, the health of our Planet, and a harmonious global community living in balance with the rhythms and cycles of all life. The Ancient Wisdom of Shamanic Medicine teachings offers powerful and beautiful ways of creating harmony, balance, and ‘ right relationship ‘ with ourselves, our community, and our planet. As we focus on creating harmony with in our own being we enable harmony to evolve and flourish in the world we create around us. Ancient Wisdom teachings guide us to remember and undertake ‘The Hero’s journey of the soul. ‘ Un conditioning ourselves back to source, reclaiming our rainbow bodies and taking our place as peaceful, luminous rainbow warriors.

Medicine Wheel.

The Medicine Wheel is a profound journey travelling through the four directions.

In The South we explore our personal history and begin to ‘ unbind’ ourselves from The stories that limit and restrict us. We challenge our old identity and the rigid structures and self limiting behaviour patterns which keep us small. We begin to loosen energy held in the etheric bodies and create space for something new to manifest. We remember the path of beauty and connect with the archetypal energy of Serpent. The foundation rites of passage are gifted in the South direction.

In the West we explore our shadow, karma, and Ancestral history . We begin to reduce the momentum of the past and free ourselves from it’s weight and influence. We allow ourselves to surrender old beliefs and karmic burdens that no longer serve us. Honouring our Ancestors, hearing their voices and receiving their gifts , we free them and ourselves to move forward towards and fulfil our true destiny. We remember the path of the luminous warrior and connect with the archetypical energy of Jaguar. The rites of pampamisayuk are gifted in the West.

In the North we surrender the roles that have defined us and begin to reclaim our power. We explore new possibilities and embrace the beauty and simplicity of our soul energy, connecting to the true joy, sweetness and nectar of life. As we move into stillness and embrace ‘beginner’s mind’ we begin to make the ‘ The Hero’s journey ‘ of the soul. Making the impossible, possible. We journey with integrity to reclaim the wisdom of The sacred Mountains as we surrender to the archetypal energy of Hummingbird. The rites of Altumisayuk are gifted in The North.

In The East we are called to transcend our old stories and beliefs and are invited to die to our old way of being in the world. We are reminded that life is not ‘ personal ‘ and limited to form. We reconnect to the vast formless realm of consciousness which is our true being. Here we remember that there is always a bigger picture and that we are participating in creation on a global and cosmic level. We journey into pure consciousness and source our new becomings from the realm of spirit. Soaring with the archetypal energy of Eagle, we are offered the opportunity to live from our Hearts. Here we can dream a new world into being as we fly wing tip to wing tip with Great Spirit. The rites of Kurak Akulleq are gifted in the East.

The Medicine Wheel teachings are offered in small group of up to 8 people, and can also be received on a one to one basis either face to face or on line if required.

One to one sessions

These sessions are offered to anyone who feels called to explore the Ancient Medicine teachings on a one to one basis rather than in a group. One to one sessions provide an opportunity for those who feel called to develop a deeper understanding of their relationship with themselves, their community, and the Earth to enjoy a connection with The Medicine teachings.

These sessions are made available for personal healing work, sharing the Medicine teachings and exploring the use of ceremony and other Shamanic practises to bring balance, harmony and healing on all levels. A one to one session may take place, face to face, or on line, depending on the circumstances and requirements of the individual.

Medicine Circles

Small groups offered to explore and utilised Medicine wisdom and teachings. This space provides the opportunity to: deepen your relationship with the elements and archetypal energies, explore the practical application of: despacho, fire ceremony, sand paintings, mandorlas, Shamanic journeying, and other Shamanic practises , to create alignment balance and harmony within your own being and the world in which you live. These groups aim to develop and provide you with simple and powerful tools to co create harmoniously with all life. They serve as an introduction to the Medicine practises as well as to support and enhance exsisting practises.
